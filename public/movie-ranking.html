Top 3 Anime Movies of all time

Your Name

Makoto Shinkai's Your Name (2016) is a visually breathtaking, emotional masterpiece. It flawlessly blends body-swap comedy, time-travel romance, and disaster drama, as a Tokyo boy and a country girl magically trade places and fight to alter a tragic fate.

Weathering with You

Weathering With You (2019), directed by Makoto Shinkai, is widely praised for its breathtaking, hyperrealistic animation and sweeping musical score by the band RADWIMPS. While some critics found the narrative slightly predictable compared to its predecessor Your Name, many admire its deeper thematic focus on climate change and youthful rebellion.

The Girl Who Leapt Through Time

Directed by Mamoru Hosoda, The Girl Who Leapt Through Time (2006) is a charming, coming-of-age sci-fi film about a clumsy high schooler who discovers she can literally jump backward in time. It brilliantly swaps epic, universe-saving heroics for relatable adolescent fears, beautifully balancing hilarious slice-of-life comedy with poignant, emotional depth.
